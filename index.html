    Management Information System (MIS) is An information system collects, processes, stores, analyzes and delivers information for a specific purpose. In short, it gets the right information to the right people, at the right time, in the right amount, and in the right format. Currently, MIS specialists are in very high demand, as they help companies find solutions that combine elements of management, organization, and technology.
